Project 1: The character behind the voice

I got inspiration from seeing collage art of people putting cartoon faces on top of other peoples faces that match up, so I thought I would take iconic voice actors and overlay one of their characters over their face. We always see the characters, but never the people behind them. Click on a profile of one of the voice actors and the image plus an audio clip of how they developed their character will show up/play. Voice actors in order from left to right Tom Kenny, Spongebob Walk Disney, Mickey Mouse Charles Martinet, Mario Rodger Criag Smith, Sonic

Project Two: Cicada

For my project 2 hyperlink narrative, I made a non-linear narrative going through my favorite poem, "Cicada" by Hosho McCreesh. In the hyperlink narrative, you have to navigate through different links, and do different interactive activities; all while going through the theme's of reflection, transformation, and growth. My biggest challenge was getting all of the transitions within my HTML to work, and not interfere with the others. Another challenge I had was putting in background sound. For some reason, it doesn't like when sound is automatically playing on the site, and so I tried to find different ways to cheat this. I tried to connect it to when the mouse moved, or when something in the page had a transition. However, I found that sound was most successful when it was in between effects, or had a direct interaction with the mouse. So, in some places where I wanted more ambience noise, I wasn't able to achieve it, because I couldn't find a way to add the sound without disrupting the visuals in the timeframe
