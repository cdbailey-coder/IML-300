ORGANIZATIONS TECHNOLOGY SUPPORT
Deaf & Hard-of-Hearing Organizations
National and international advocacy groups working to support the D/HH community
  • NATIONAL ASSOCIATION OF THE DEAF (NAD)
    www.nad.org
    Leading civil rights organization for deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals in the United States
  • HEARING LOSS ASSOCIATION OF AMERICA
    www.hearingloss.org
    Provides support, information, and resources for people with hearing loss
  • WORLD FEDERATION OF THE DEAF
    www.wfdeaf.org
    International organization representing deaf people worldwide
Assistive Technology
Tools and devices to improve communication and accessibility
  • HEARING AIDS & COCHLEAR IMPLANTS
    Amplification devices and surgical implants to improve hearing capability
  • SPEECH-TO-TEXT APPS
    Real-time transcription tools like Otter.ai, Google Live Transcribe, and Ava
  • CAPTIONING SOFTWARE
    Closed captioning services for videos, meetings, and live events
  • VIDEO RELAY SERVICES (VRS)
    Communication services allowing deaf users to make phone calls via sign language interpreters
Mental Health Resources
Accessible mental health support for the D/HH community
  • ACCESSIBLE THERAPY SERVICES
    Therapists and counselors trained in working with D/HH clients, offering ASL fluency or captioning
  • TEXT/VIDEO CRISIS HOTLINES
    24/7 support lines accessible via text, video relay, or online chat
  • ONLINE PLATFORMS WITH ASL SUPPORT
    Digital mental health platforms offering captioning and sign language interpretation
