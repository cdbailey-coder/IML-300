Live Captioning

Real-time speech-to-text apps like Otter.ai and Google Live Transcribe enable D/HH individuals to follow conversations in meetings, classes, and social settings.

+

Video Relay Services

VRS allows sign language users to communicate with hearing people via video interpreters, bridging communication gaps in real-time.

+

Assistive Devices

Hearing aids, cochlear implants, and FM systems amplify sound and improve communication in various environments.

+

Alert Systems

Visual and vibration alerts for doorbells, phones, and emergencies ensure D/HH individuals stay connected and safe.

Peer Support Groups

Deaf Community Centers

Physical and virtual spaces for D/HH culture, events, and social gatherings that foster belonging.

Mentorship Programs

Online Communities

Digital forums and social media groups connecting D/HH individuals globally, reducing geographic isolation.
