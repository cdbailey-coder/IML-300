Exercise 1: Face

This is my first exercise. Your cursor is a hairdryer drying the figure's hair. I was inspired by the artist Irene Rinaldi, and her minimalistic designs using simple shapes. I want to add a bit more detail to the face and clean up some lines that become exposed as the hair moves. My biggest challenge was isolating different features so they wouldn't be affected by other pieces of code's scale, movement, etc. The formatting feels messy, but I'm sure it will get cleaner with practice!

Exercise 2: Drawing Tool

I was inspired by seeing pictures of children's play mats with the roads on them. I made a build your own town play mat where you draw the road and then add features like: people, cars, buildings, trees, etc. I really enjoyed the playful nature of this. The biggest thing I would change, but couldn't figure out, is to have an undo button in case you place something you didn't mean to. I would also like to make it so the straight road parts drag with your mouse in a straight line, since it's kind of hard to make a straight line.

Exercise 3: Interactive Pattern

I was inspired by the pattern and color shifts of kaleidoscopes. Every time you click on the canvas, the color scheme changes.
